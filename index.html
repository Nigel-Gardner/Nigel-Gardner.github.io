About

Thanks for taking the time to read about my background. I am a proud US Veteran who is a former Marine Corp Sergeant for 8 years. I then received my ROTC Commission from Fordham University and have spent the last 8 years as a US Army Captain. I have served multiple tours overseas including Iraq, Afghanistan, Japan and Norway. With these opportunities, I have been fortunate to work with a diverse group and team that has given me a good perspective on cultures and values.

I am a driven professional who embraces new technology with proven ability to handle multiple projects and make targeted deadlines. I am also a team oriented person who has the ability to thrive in any environment, accomplish all tasks and exceed expectations. I am a quick learner and good troubleshooter who can provide feedback and recommendations on how to handle issues as they arise.

Prior to making this successful transition into web development I spent over 10 years in operations and project management, audit and compliance.

I spent the majority of my time analyzing data, researching best practices and implementing various programs to improve business productivity. With the rapid shift to web based technology, I constantly found myself researching and relying on technology to automate our processes and increase efficiency and client satisfaction. I look forward to achieving the same success and applying these same skills as a full stack web developer.

Ski Planner

For my first project, I built a full-stack web application that centered around skiing since it is one of my favorite hobbies. 
The application will allow you to create your own profile with encrypted login, add locations you have or will want to visit, get weather updates for those locations and invite friends to your trip. 
For the client-side, the app uses JavaScript, and the HTML templateMustache.js. For the server-side I am running Express.js with jQuery for the AJAX call.
